WHO WE Are

The nineties generation has been structuring and is now considered a helping hand in what has become the digital age of today. The 1990s stand as the meeting point of Millennials & Gen Z. The generation that witnessed the beginning of the online world and the generation that was born into the online world. At 199X, we're a young passionate team mostly born in the nineties. We offer our clients creative, youthful strategies and campaigns that work alongside the current market trends. Moreover, we help brands become trendsetters themselves. The X in "Nineteen Ninety ex" stands for the different YOBs, but also stands for the significant impact that we bring to brands & services.

OUR BELIEFS

Any agency or brand can stir up endless white noise forproducts and services, but this does no one any good. Even after this approach inevitably fails, the reflex is often to"turn up" the noise for better results rather than fine-tune it. At 199X, observing this approach has confirmed a long-heldsuspicion: just because an idea is louder or flashier, thatdoesn't mean the message is being heard. To be heard, wehave to be smarter, not louder. This is an expertly crafteddisruption. If an idea isn't disruptive or engaging enough tocatch consumer attention, it's only adding to the white noise inthe background.
